Consequences of Poor Payroll Management

Manual payroll errors, incorrect deductions, and late filings of statutory returns result in steep regulatory penalties and operational chaos:

  • TDS Late Filing Fees: Delay in submitting quarterly Form 24Q (Salary TDS) results in a mandatory penalty of ₹200 per day under Section 234E of the Income Tax Act.
  • PF/ESI Auditing Damages: Incorrect calculations of wage caps and delayed monthly return filings trigger interest (12% p.a.) and damages (up to 25% p.a.).
  • Loss of Employee Trust: Inconsistent payouts, tax deduction errors, and delayed payslips lead to labor friction and increased employee attrition.
